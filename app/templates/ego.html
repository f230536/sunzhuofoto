Images, seem to me, come out as a matter of nature. Photographing is not so much a recording process as a ceaseless procedure. Essentially, what most important thing to shooting is experiencing and enjoying the procedure. Based on the flow of self-consciousness and exploration of identity, I wish more works will be created by photographic language implications, coupled with different media and techniques. Meantime, I also want to cover further distance in exploring the relations between “internal image” and “external image” and the ways of their presentation.
